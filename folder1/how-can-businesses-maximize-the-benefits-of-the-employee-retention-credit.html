How can businesses maximize the benefits of the employee retention credit?

The IRS allows businesses to claim an additional tax credit of up to $1,000 for each full-time employee they retain for the tax year. The credit is available to both small businesses and large corporations that have met certain criteria to qualify. This credit helps reduce your taxes if you hire and retain employees. It’s one of the most powerful tax incentives for retaining employees and can be used in tandem with other hiring incentives like an employee stock options or a cash bonus. If you hire employees who qualify for health insurance under the Affordable Care Act (ACA), the credit can also be used to help pay for that coverage.
